About Falcon's

Brief club history.

Transnzoia Football Team was founded on the desire of the founders to reach to the rural youths in Trans-Nzoia County that are marginalized to empower them and improve social economic and leadership status in the community, understand their well being especially and take control of their lives economically and reach them with information about the importance of nurturing and playing and effects of drugs and substances use to youths and link them to institutions supporting the services.

Team Vision & and Mission

#

1. Empowering youths and spear heading education, awareness and sensitization on the effects of drugs and substance abuse towards exercising their social economic and cultural rights freely in the community.


2. Empowering and nurturing girls football in the slums to help in the prevention of drug and substance abuse amongst the youths.
