Trans-Nzoia Falcons

Women Team & Empowerment platform.

Club Mission Statement.

Mission

Transnzoia Falcons Football Team exists to reach out to the vulnerable women/girls, people/groups with knowledge and skills that can transform their lives, increase their capacity to tackle life’s challenges for a transformed society within Trans-Nzoia County..

Vision

Empowering the female youth through football.

Club Management.

This is a brief descripton of how our team management is organized.

Transnzoia Falcons Football Team is governed by the Team’s Patron and Matron, Team’s Chairman, Finance Officer, Team Manager, Public Relation Officer, Secretary, Head Coach, Assisant coaches. All the team’s finance is managed by the finance officer by advice of the chairman and legal advisers. The Team has several internal control systems such as the cash analysis book, the payment vouchers, and the check books. Auditing will be done by both internal and external auditors and the grant will be strictly maintained to the project requested for. The organization has the financial management policies and the human resource policy that helps in its management. Below is the group structure of governance.
